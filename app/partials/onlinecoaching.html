What happens if we are a match?

Once I decide to take you on as a client and you decide that you are 100% ready and committed to this process, you will purchase the online coaching bundle (8 or 12 weeks) and I will get started on your unique training program and nutrition plan.

I may have additional or follow-up questions for you.

Your 8 or 12 weeks of coaching starts after I finish preparing your plan and we decide together on your start date. At that point I will send you your first week of your training plan and you have will have 2-3 days time to review before you begin. This gives you time to ask questions and receive answers on the movements.

I will require 7 days/week for your training plan and activity goals. Depending on your starting fitness level and goals some of these days may be "free activity days" where I will require only a certain amount of activity but YOU get to choose what that looks like (i.e. walking at the park or on a treadmill for 30 minutes, yoga, biking with your kids).

I will require you to track your food 100% of the time in My Fitness Pal so that I can follow along and see exactly what is going on with your food intake. This is very important! We will tweak calories and macros as needed on your journey! You will need a small food scale and these are easy and cheap to purchase on Amazon!

If you have the desire to hide food from me, don't. I am not here to judge, I am here to help and if you hide I cannot help. If I know you are struggling with the plan then I can adjust to help you get what you need (an occasional "off the plan" meal). I WILL work with your lifestyle and needs, because I want this to become your longterm way of life, and a change for a lifetime. Sidenote: if you LOVE pizza, we will make it happen!!!

What happens if I have questions about my training and nutrition?

I will respond to e-mails within one workday (usually much sooner).

Make sure to review your training plan in depth before you go workout and plan accordingly. If you have questions about a workout ask me those questions 24 hours before you do the workout so I can respond and answer all your questions!

Feel free to e-mail videos for me to check your form, it is very important to me!!

I will also send videos to help with a visual on form or I will connect a Youtube video that shows the correct form.

Do I need to purchase any quipment?

You will need a small food scale, which you can find on Amazon easily.

You will need access to a body weight scale which you will be able to consistently use at the same time and same place every week.

You will need something to measure your body fat % (if you have a gym membership usually they will let you use their handheld one if you ask), or you can have someone skilled use a skin fold measurement device.

I can make suggestions for extra equipment to buy for home workouts but if you want body weight workouts with no equipment, then that is what I will make happen for you.

What if I want you to coach me longer than 12 weeks?

I would LOVE that!

If you want to continue coaching, you will simply purchase another 8 or 12 week package and we will continue on in your journey together!

Will I have weekly check-ins?

YES!

We will decide on which day is the best for your weekly check-in, on check-in day you will send me updated weigh-in and pictures updates.

You will send me a summary of how your week went, physically, mentally, and emotionally.

You will send me any questions you have regarding your plan.

You will express any frustrations with your plan or yourself so that I can tweak things to help you STICK to it!

I will review, and change your next week's plan accordingly and respond to any questions or comments you have.
