About Me

My Story

When I began my fitness journey I wasn't the strong and athletically inclined girl. I wasn't a high school athlete or a childhood gymnast. After college I decided I was sick of never looking the way I wanted to look and eating 1200 calories a day in muffins while trying so hard to get over my hatred of cardio. I finally decided to try a strength training program and committed to consistently lifting weights for one year.

As I grew to enjoy my workouts and learned how to have better form, I found myself growing in confidence, eating healthier, and eating more. I was stronger, leaner, and more energetic. Before I knew what had happened, I wasn't obsessing over the scale anymore and I was living a healthy and balanced life. Physical activity was now a part of my life that I didn't want to skip and it wasn't a chore. I GOT to workout, instead of "I HAVE to workout."

It became clear that my passion in life was to help others in their fitness journey.
